Which Traits Do You Have?

  For each of these four traits choose the one you typically lean more towards.


Introvert People with a preference for Introversion draw energy from an internal world of thought and reflection. Or Extrovert People with a preference for Extraversion draw energy from an external world of interaction and doing.
Sensor Seeking the richest possible experience that is grounded in reality and through sensory input. Or Intuitive Seeking the richest possible experience that explores information through deep understanding, meaning-making, relationships, patterns.
Feeling Naturally use personal feelings and values when making decisions. Relationship focused. Or Thinking Naturally search for principles and logic when making decisions. Outcome/goal focused.
Judging Often appear planned and organized in their daily routines. Feel supported by structure and guidelines. Or Perceiving Often appear to have no plan of action and act on spontaneity. Flexible, adaptable and enjoy freedom and variety.

You should now be left with four traits. Take the first letter of each trait and this will be your Myers-Briggs type.
